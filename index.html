01 · About

I like building software that does something real.

I'm an AI student in Kakinada who builds the plumbing around the model, not just the model. A chatbot that also talks back. A dashboard that also flips a relay on the other side of the world. That's the part of AI I actually enjoy — making it usable, not just demo-able.

Backend is Python and Flask. Frontend is plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript. When a project needs to leave the browser, I reach for ESP32/ESP8266. Right now that's an AI video pipeline and a punching bag that scores your form.

  • Ship end-to-end — I own a project from backend logic to the deployed UI, not just one layer of it.
  • Comfortable crossing the software/hardware line — Flask on one side, ESP32 firmware on the other.
  • Debug by narrowing, not guessing — every project here has a "what broke and how I found it" story.
